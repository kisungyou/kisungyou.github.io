Welcome!

I am a postdoctoral associate in the Department of Internal Medicine at Yale School of Medicine working with Prof. Dennis Shung. My research focuses on the use of mathematical tools from statistics, geometry, and topology in developing novel methods to answer abstruse scientific questions. The primary application domain is biomedical data analysis. I am also deeply interested in high-performance computing and statistical software development to democratize methodological innovations for practitioners.
